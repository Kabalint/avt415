P. P1. 1: Idea

Idea #1: Custom Art Apparel Store

A store for custom, non-licensed apparel made to support smaller artists, who receive a cut of each sale. Most designs are pop culture themed, and the fun modern site theming reflects this.

Idea #2: Grocery Store

One of the smaller grocery stores that does not have wide appeal, but wins over devout customers with better prices and quality items. It's nicheness is embraced by fun, non-corporate branding.

Inspiration Site

Idea #3: Record Store

A home for the medium of physical music including cds, vinyl, and their respective devices. It will embrace the retro roots of its subject matter and feature a very 80s "movie theater carpet pattern" vibe.
